There is a process that happens when making a YouTube video. The first step is making the video itself. The first thing you must do is record the actual video. If you are doing a type of in real life video you would need a camera,preferably atleast one with an SD card and now your phone. If you would like to record something on your computer, there are programs like bandicam that can record the whole screen, or DxTory that records games. For this page I am going to go through the process of making YouTube videos on your computer.


As said before, to record on the computer you should either get a full screen recorder or a game recorder depending on what you want to film. There are many guides on how and where to download them, so I'm not going to get into that. After you have your video recording program downloaded, you need to learn how to use it. Every program is different, so you need to learn how to use the program you have. Again, there are many guides out there to show you how to use these programs so I am not going to explain how every recording software works. Now that you have your recording softwaree, now it is time to decide what to record! After you have decided what to record, like you might think, it is time to actually record! From here you need to use your best judgement and ask yourself, "Is this a good enough video"? If not, try and refilming, if it is good eneough, you are ready for the next step. This next step is optional but highly reccomended. This step is edditing. Just like the recording software, there are many edditting softwares out there and many guides on how to use them, so use your internet searching skills and look for a good edditting program and find out how to use it!


Now that you have the video you want, now its time to actually upload it. Assuming that you have a youtube account (if not you should be able to google or find out how),you will see an upload button on the top right. Once you click on that button it will bring you to a page where you will easily be able to see where to drag your video file. After dragging your video file in and clicking upload you will have to wait a certain time period for the video to be uploaded to the site. Uploading the video can take anywhere from 10 minutes to 48hours depending on how long the video is and how fast your internet is.


Once your video is uploaded you will be brought to a page with a bunch of options. These options can include video privacy, description, likes, and title. The video privacy option includes private, public, and semi-private. The private option will make sure that only your account can view the video. The semi-private option will ensure that only persons with the direct link can view the video. The public optoin allows anybody around the world to search up the video and view it. You are also able to customize the title and description to whatever you like by simply typing into the think box labeled title and bigger box labled description.


There you have it! You now have your very own youtube video!
